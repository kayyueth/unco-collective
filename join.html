We are recruiting Research Members for Sprint 02, forming June 2026. We want 5–8 people who will actually do the work. The gap between "cool idea" and "I will write 2,000 words in three weeks" is the central test of this whole experiment.

§ 01 · What We're Looking For

A working researcher.

The ideal Research Member is someone already doing the work — an academic mid-way through a dissertation, a practitioner writing on the side, a translator with a body of work, a policy analyst with opinions. What matters is track record and willingness to ship.

  • You have written something serious

    A thesis, a paper, an essay, a long technical post, a translation. Anything with a point of view and a bibliography.

  • You can commit to a six-week sprint

    This is the hard one. Six weeks of disciplined, time-boxed work. Weekly sync calls. Drafts by the deadline. No exceptions.

  • You work well with three to seven other people

    Cells fragment when members hoard, withdraw, or moralize. We want generous, opinionated collaborators.

  • You have a position on something

    We publish propositions, not summaries. If you cannot name a thesis you want to argue for, this is not the right fit.

§ 04 · Frequently Asked

Do I need to be based in Asia?

No. The collective is remote-first and has members across Hong Kong, Taipei, Singapore, Shanghai, London, and New York. Weekly sync calls rotate across timezones.

Do I need to speak Chinese?

No. English is the primary working language. Chinese translation and dissemination is done by members with that capacity.

Is there compensation?

Yes, where funding allows. 40% of sponsored and advisory revenue is distributed to contributors. Donor-funded papers include a stipend for the lead author. Early sprints are lightly funded; this will change as the collective matures.

What if I want to fund a paper?

Write to partners@uncommons.xyz. We accept sponsored research subject to our editorial independence clause.

How do I just follow along?

Subscribe to the newsletter (link in the footer). Papers and salons are announced there.
