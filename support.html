Uncommons is sustained through individual patronage, institutional grants, and sponsored research. Every dollar funds rigorous, independent research on emerging internet institutions. No venture capital. Editorial independence non-negotiable.

§ 01 · Why Support

Research needs
patrons.

Uncommons produces public-interest research without a business model. We are not a startup, a nonprofit, or a think tank. We are a collective of working researchers funded by the people and institutions that believe the work matters.

Your support funds:

  • Researcher stipends — so writers can spend time on rigorous work instead of just getting paid
  • Translation & dissemination — making research legible across languages and audiences
  • Peer review & editorial — maintaining standards independent of funders
  • Tools & infrastructure — shared workspace, collaborative writing, publication platforms
  • A research seed fund — supporting exploratory work and junior researchers

§ 03 · Sponsor a Paper

Commission research
on a thesis.

Foundations, protocols, and institutions can sponsor Uncommons to produce research on a specific topic. Sponsored papers carry an editorial independence clause: funders propose topics, but do not control conclusions.

Sponsored research typically costs $8,000–$15,000 depending on scope and timeline. This covers researcher time, peer review, translation, and dissemination.

§ 05 · Frequently Asked

What happens if my funding ends?

Funding is month-to-month. You can pause or cancel anytime. No penalty, no questions. Papers already produced remain published and open-access.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Uncommons is not currently a registered nonprofit. We are exploring 501(c)(3) status in the US and equivalent structures in other jurisdictions. Individual donations are not tax-deductible at this stage, but institutional grants may be. Consult your accountant.

How is money used?

40% goes to contributors (lead authors, peer reviewers, editors). 30% funds operations and reserves. 20% funds dissemination (translation, design, events). 10% seeds new research. We publish detailed allocation quarterly.

Can I sponsor a specific paper?

Yes. Sponsored papers have a budget of $8k–$15k and come with an editorial independence clause. Write to partners@uncommons.xyz.

What if I want to fund something unusual?

Let's talk. Email hello@uncommons.xyz with your idea. We fund research, not propaganda — but the range of what counts as research is wide.
